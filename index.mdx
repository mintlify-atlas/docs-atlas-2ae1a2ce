Everything runs in your browser: no backend, no registration, no data collection.

## What is ACGTI?

ACGTI maps your answers through four MBTI dimensions (E/I, S/N, T/F, J/P) into one of **8 ACG personality archetypes**, then narrows the result down to a single character from a curated library of 42 characters. Results include interactive trait sliders, a character code, and a shareable PNG poster.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Answer 39 questions">
    Each question presents a real scenario. Respond on a 7-point scale from strong agreement to strong disagreement. There are no right or wrong answers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your archetype">
    The scoring engine calculates your position across four MBTI dimensions and maps you to one of 8 anime personality archetypes — from Luminous Lead to Shadow Strategist.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Meet your character match">
    A vector-similarity algorithm identifies the single character from the library who best mirrors your unique dimension profile and archetype tendencies.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share your result">
    Export a beautifully designed PNG poster and share your character code with friends.
  </Step>
</Steps>
