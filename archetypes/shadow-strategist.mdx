The most decisive moments in a story often look, in hindsight, like they were inevitable. For the Shadow Strategist, they were. You've already read the field, mapped the outcomes, and positioned your pieces while everyone else is still sizing up the situation. You don't announce your plans. You don't need to. When the board turns, it turns the way you arranged it.

## 影面策士 — Shadow Strategist

> 你会先考虑全局，再决定自己要站在什么位置。
