The front line gets the attention. The boundary line keeps everyone safe. The Moonlit Guardian is the archetype that holds position at the edge — not seeking visibility, not needing recognition, but always there at the moment the perimeter matters. You're not the lightning strike. You're the light that was already on when everything else went dark, and will still be on when the scene settles.

## 月下守护者 — Moonlit Guardian

> 你不是冲到前面最亮的那一个，但你会一直守着重要的人和事。

**Subtitle:** 安静站在边界上的人 *(The one standing quietly at the boundary)*

**Narrative role:** 护卫位 / 结界位

**Accent color:** <Badge color="blue">#90caf9</Badge>

**MBTI types:** INFP · ISTJ · ISFP
