Leadership isn't always about volume. The Oathbound Captain is the person who doesn't need to be the loudest in the room to be the most essential. You shoulder the weight of the group's direction, you hold formation when everything is pulling apart, and you're still at your post after everyone else has stepped back. Responsibility isn't a burden you picked up — it's something you naturally walked toward.

## 誓约队长 — Oathbound Captain
