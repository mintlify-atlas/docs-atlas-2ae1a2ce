Some stories only start moving when someone refuses to follow the script. The Chaos Spark is exactly that someone. You don't enter a scene quietly, and you don't leave it the way it was. Your presence accelerates things — not because you're trying to cause disruption, but because you're drawn to the alive edge of a situation, where the outcome is still undecided and the energy is crackling. You make the story happen.
