The script is rarely as locked in as it seems. The Trickster Orbit is the character who notices the gap in the established pattern and slips right through it — not out of rebellion for its own sake, but because you see angles no one else is watching. When a situation locks up and everyone starts running the same play, you're already three steps sideways, setting up a pivot the room didn't see coming.

## 灵巧回旋者 — Trickster Orbit

> 你不一定按常规出牌，但你总能把剧情转出新角度。

**Subtitle:** 总能把局面绕出新花样 *(Always finding a new spin on the situation)*
