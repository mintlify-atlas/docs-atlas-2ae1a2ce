Not every essential role comes with a spotlight. The Gentle Healer is the character who doesn't grab attention but whose absence would hollow out the group — the person who holds emotional space, notices when someone is close to breaking, and offers exactly the support needed before anyone thinks to ask. Your warmth isn't incidental. It's structural.

## 温柔修复者 — Gentle Healer

> 你不是最耀眼的，但你总能给人最坚实的依靠。

**Subtitle:** 把裂缝慢慢缝回去的人 *(The one who slowly stitches the cracks closed)*

**Narrative role:** 疗愈位 / 情感中继
