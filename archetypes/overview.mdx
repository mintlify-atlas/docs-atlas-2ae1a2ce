The ACG Type Indicator sorts quiz results into eight narrative archetypes drawn from classic anime tropes. Each archetype captures a distinct way of moving through a story — how loudly you speak, how warmly you connect, how tightly you hold to order, and what role you naturally fill when the plot thickens. Whether you burn bright at the front line or keep a quiet watch at the boundary, there is a place in the cast for you.

## MBTI-to-archetype mapping

The quiz engine maps each of the 16 MBTI types to one archetype via `TYPE_TO_ARCHETYPE` in `quizEngine.ts`. The table below shows the full mapping.

| Archetype | Narrative role | MBTI types |
|---|---|---|
| Luminous Lead | 主角团核心 / 气氛引擎 | ENFJ, ESFJ |
| Icebound Observer | 军师位 / 冷静旁白 | INTP, ISTP |
| Oathbound Captain | 队长位 / 编队中轴 | ENTJ, ESTJ |
| Trickster Orbit | 副主角 / 破局位 | ENTP, ENFP |
| Gentle Healer | 疗愈位 / 情感中继 | INFJ, ISFJ |
| Shadow Strategist | 幕后操盘 / 黑箱智囊 | INTJ |
| Chaos Spark | 转折触发器 / 爆点制造机 | ESTP, ESFP |
| Moonlit Guardian | 护卫位 / 结界位 | INFP, ISTJ, ISFP |

<Note>
The quiz blends MBTI scoring with archetype-weighted answers and a six-axis personality vector. Your final result reflects the full weighted calculation, not just your four-letter type.
</Note>

## How the vector axes work

Every archetype carries a signature profile across six dimensions. These scores shape character matching and result weighting.

| Axis | What it measures |
|---|---|
| `expression` | How openly you communicate and take up space |
| `temperature` | Emotional warmth versus cool detachment |
| `judgement` | Analytical precision and pattern recognition |
| `order` | Preference for structure, rules, and stability |
| `agency` | Drive to act, lead, and initiate |
| `aura` | Presence, gravity, and the pull you exert on others |
