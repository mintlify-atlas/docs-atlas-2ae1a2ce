Some people don't wait to be invited into the spotlight — they just step forward and the light follows. The Luminous Lead is the anime protagonist energy made explicit: the person who takes the first step when everyone else is still hesitating, who keeps the group's rhythm alive, and who makes the scene feel like something worth watching. You're not just a background character. You move the plot.
