ACGTI (ACG Type Indicator) is an open-source, browser-only personality quiz that draws on the MBTI framework to match you with an anime character archetype. Answer 39 scenario-based questions on a seven-point scale, and the engine maps your responses across four psychological dimensions to land you in one of eight narrative archetypes and, ultimately, one specific character from a library of 42 — spanning titles like VOCALOID, Evangelion, Detective Conan, Touhou, Bocchi the Rock, MyGO, and Genshin Impact.

Everything runs locally in your browser. No account, no backend, no data collection.

<Warning>
  ACGTI is for entertainment purposes only. Results do not constitute psychological diagnosis, medical evaluation, or any professional personality assessment. Treat your result as a fun conversation starter, not a definitive self-profile.
</Warning>

## What you get

- **39 scenario questions** — each scored on a −3 to +3 Likert scale and weighted across MBTI dimensions and archetype roles simultaneously
- **8 exclusive archetypes** — Luminous Lead, Icebound Observer, Oathbound Captain, Trickster Orbit, Gentle Healer, Shadow Strategist, Chaos Spark, and Moonlit Guardian
- **42-character library** — 40 standard characters plus 2 hidden ones, drawn from a wide range of popular ACG franchises, with more added regularly
- **Shareable result poster** — export your result as a PNG image to share with friends, styled after the 16personalities interactive trait-bar layout
- **Pure frontend** — the entire scoring pipeline runs in the browser using Vue 3 and TypeScript; nothing is ever sent to a server

<Note>
  ACGTI passed 4 million visits in April 2026, just days after going public. The project is MIT-licensed and welcomes contributions — new characters, new questions, and bug fixes alike.
</Note>
